竹 Bamboo 氵 Tsunami 丶 Drop 専 Specialty

Meaning
Primary
Record Book

Mnemonic
A bamboo tsunami uses its drops to carry out its specialty, which is turning itself into record books.

Hints

The bamboo tsunami hits the ground and drops of bamboo turn into sheets of paper. The sheets of paper combine to become record books that record what happened here, on this day.

Readings
On’yomi

ぼ

Kun’yomi

None

Nanori

None

Mnemonic
A record book is a book (ぼ), when it comes down to it.

Hints

Bamboo can also become bo staffs too, if they don't turn into record books.
