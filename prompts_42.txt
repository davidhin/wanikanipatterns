柄 Pattern

The kanji is composed of three radicals. Can you see where the radicals fit in the kanji?
木 Tree 丙 Dynamite

Meaning
Primary
Pattern

Mnemonic
You walk up to a tree and put dynamite on it. Then you set it off. BOOM. It explodes, but doesn't do what you'd expect. Instead of blowing the tree up, it blasts a very beautiful pattern into the side of it as well as the ground around it.

Hints

Think about what pattern it might be. Is it a checkered pattern? Something more complicated and beautiful? Whatever it is, think of something that really impresses you.

Readings
On’yomi

へい

Kun’yomi

がら

Nanori

None

Mnemonic
The pattern is so beautiful that you want to put it on your garage (がら). So, you do.

Hints

Imagine painting your garage with the pattern you imagined earlier.
